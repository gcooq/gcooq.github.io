Qiang Gao

Ph.D. at SWUFE· qianggao@swufe.edu.cn

Qiang Gao is currently working as the Lecture at the School of Economic Information Engineering, Southwestern University of Finance and Economics (西南财经大学, SWUFE) since March 2021. Qiang Gao received the Ph.D. degree in the Software Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (电子科技大学, UESTC), Chengdu, Sichuan, China. Qiang Gao was a visiting scholar at Northwestern University from 2019 to 2020.
